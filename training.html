Training


We offer on site fork lift training using the latest training techniques, combining theory and practical exercises in accordance with health and safety guidelines.
Fork lift training on site has the advantage of operators being familiar with the actual equipment and types of loads they will be using on a daily basis in the working environment.
There is no wasted travel time or expense for the delegates.
We train on all types of truck's; counterbalance, reach and pallet. We also offer courses for novice operators as well as refreshers and conversions.
